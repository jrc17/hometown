Visit Bengaluru

Enjoy nature, food and culture in this vibrant city, the capital of Karnataka.

Top three activities to do at Bengaluru

Go for a picnic at Cubbon Park

The Cubbon Park is perfect for picnic, enjoy nature and just chilling out.

Go shopping and eat great food at MG Road

The MG Road is a great place to street shop and eat out.

Play at Wonderla

Enjoy water rides and land rides at the amusement park, Wonderla

Top three foods to try out at Bengaluru

Set dosa

Thick, soft and spongy, these dosas come in a set of three served along with sagu or coconut chutney

Masala Puri

A delicious street food from Bangalore. The broth is made from dried peas and served on a plate of puris. A must have

Rice Bath

A flavourful, aromatic and tasty rice dish serverd along with raita or coconut chutney
